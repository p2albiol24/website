My Hero: Neil deGrasse Tyson









Neil deGrasse Tyson is a world-known astrophysicist who has starred on many American documentaries
and television shows on the subject of extra-planetary education. Born in Manhattan in 1958,
Tyson always had a fascination of space and planetary bodies. At age 9, he visited the Hayden
Planetarium, and reportedly felt that the skies were calling to him. During high school, he
became well known at the planetarium for his frequent visits and even enrolled in an extracurricular
class. He later became an undergraduate from Harvard with a degree in physics, and finished off
in UTA. One of his many talents include being able to describe extremely complex ideas, concepts, and
theories in simple terms that young children can understand.



Why I chose Neil. Well in my opinion, he is an awesome guy with an incredibly complex mind that
he uses to enourage other people into studying the stars. I honestly have learned quite a bit from
him, just watching his lectures, speeches, and documentaries. Obviously not comparable to anything
that qualified people know, but enough to pique my interest and set me on a path to try and focus my
career towards that.
